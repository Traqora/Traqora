# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 27, 2026**

Traqora ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our flight synchronization and booking platform.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Data
While using our application, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information (PII) that can be used to contact or identify you. This includes:
- Email address
- First name and last name
- Phone number
- Stellar wallet address

### 1.2 Usage Data
We may also collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our service or when you access the service by or through a mobile device.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data for various purposes:
- To provide and maintain our Service
- To notify you about changes to our Service
- To process flight bookings and payments via Stripe
- To interact with the Stellar blockchain for on-chain flight synchronization
- To provide customer support
- To gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve our Service
- To monitor the usage of our Service
- To detect, prevent and address technical issues

## 3. Data Storage and Encryption
We take security seriously. All PII (emails, names, phone numbers) are encrypted at rest in our database using industry-standard AES-256-GCM encryption.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our Service:
- **Stripe**: For payment processing. Stripe has its own Privacy Policy.
- **Stellar Network**: For decentralized flight synchronization. Blockchain transactions are public by nature.

## 5. Your Data Protection Rights (GDPR/CCPA)
Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:
- The right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you.
- The right of rectification.
- The right to object.
- The right of restriction.
- The right to data portability.
- The right to withdraw consent.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [privacy@traqora.com](mailto:privacy@traqora.com).
